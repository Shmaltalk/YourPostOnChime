Your Post On Chime

What would this tweet look like in emojis?

A question we have all asked ourselves. Well now you can find out! Enter a link to a tweet; confirm or add a description for any images, videos, or gifs in the tweet; and send it along to GPT for translation into emoijs.


Results are generated by sending your provided data into a large language model (GPT3) as part of a broader prompt. The model then produces statistically-likely tokens according to the prompt, in this case emojis. The results you receive can reflect biases of the model.
